Black Existence Films

es una productora audiovisual

independiente de afrodescendiente

Creada el 11 de mayo de 2017 por Wisny Dorce, cineasta y productor de cine independiente con experiencia en el medio desde 2014.

En Black Existencia Films promovemos la inclusión socioeconómica y la cultura de los afrodescendientes en el mundo, brindando oportunidades laborales a actores, técnicos y personal audiovisual afrodescendiente y afro inmigrante y generando contenidos para llegar a las comunidades, transmitir mensajes y así visibilizar las realidades vividas.

LOS HERMANOS AFRO

Leo and Mimi are two Afro-Argentine brothers because of their ethnic condition, they find it difficult to progress in a society that discriminates against them. They are highly motivated to change that reality: Will they be able to fulfill their dreams, defeat prejudices, and reverse the exclusion to which the system subjects them?

EL AFORTUNADO

El Afortunado is a melodramatic film that revolves around the life of Emanuel, a young Afro-Argentine who lives with his two younger brothers Emmanuela, daughter of a businessman suffering from terminal cancer. The lives of Emanuel and Emmanuela intersect when their father, before dying, mistakenly transfers the inheritance amount to Emanuel's account. Even though Emanuel is a biochemist, he can't find a job, so he needs that money. Meanwhile Emmanuela will do the impossible to recover what she belongs to, even transgressing several laws to achieve it.
